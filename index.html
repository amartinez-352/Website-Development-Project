How Rocket League Ranking Works

Understanding MMR (Matchmaking Rating)

Your rank is determined by a hidden number called MMR, not just by time played. Here's what you need to know:

  • Win to rank up: Each win gives you approximately 8-15 MMR
  • Consistency matters: Losses subtract similar amounts
  • Rank thresholds: Each rank has specific MMR ranges
  • Divisions: 3 tiers + 4 divisions per rank (except SSL)

⚡ Key Insight: Hours don't rank you up—winning consistently does.

Quick Tips for Ranking Up

1. Focus on Consistency

Flashy mechanics won't help if you can't hit the ball reliably. Master the basics first.

2. Learn Rotation

Proper positioning and rotation are more important than mechanical skill at most ranks.

3. Review Replays

Watch your games to identify mistakes. You can't fix what you don't notice.

4. Practice Daily

Even 30 minutes of focused training is better than hours of unfocused gameplay.

5. Stay Positive

Every loss is a learning opportunity. Tilt and toxicity will only slow your progress.

6. Manage Boost

Don't waste boost. Knowing when to grab small pads vs. full boosts is crucial.
