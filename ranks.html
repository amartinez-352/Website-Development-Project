Rocket League Rank Progression Guide

From Bronze to Supersonic Legend: Your Complete Roadmap

How Ranking Actually Works

MMR (Matchmaking Rating) is the hidden number that determines your rank.

  • Typical gain/loss: 8–15 MMR per game
  • Each rank has specific MMR threshold ranges
  • 3 tiers + 4 divisions per rank (except SSL)
  • Important: Hours don't rank you up—winning consistently does

Complete Rank Breakdown

🥉

Bronze

0–274 MMR

⏱️ Estimated Hours

0–50 hours

Skill Level: Brand new players

🎮 Mechanics Needed

  • Basic driving
  • Hitting the ball consistently
  • Boost usage (don't stay empty)

📈 To Rank Up → Silver

  1. Stop whiffing
  2. Learn basic positioning (don't chase constantly)
  3. Hit the ball with purpose
🥈

Silver

275–424 MMR

⏱️ Estimated Hours

50–150 hours

🎮 Mechanics Needed

  • Basic aerial attempts
  • Ball control (simple touches)
  • Faster reactions

📈 To Rank Up → Gold

  1. Start rotating (don't all chase)
  2. Improve ball contact consistency
  3. Learn simple clears
🥇

Gold

425–574 MMR

⏱️ Estimated Hours

150–300 hours

🎮 Mechanics Needed

  • Controlled hits
  • Basic aerials (some accuracy)
  • Power shots

📈 To Rank Up → Platinum

  1. Stop double committing
  2. Learn back-post rotation
  3. Improve defense consistency
💎

Platinum

575–814 MMR

⏱️ Estimated Hours

300–600 hours

🎮 Mechanics Needed

  • Faster aerials
  • Basic dribbling
  • Recoveries starting to matter

📈 To Rank Up → Diamond

  1. Learn half-flips
  2. Learn wave dashes
  3. Improve decision-making
  4. Play more team-oriented
💠

Diamond

815–1054 MMR

⏱️ Estimated Hours

600–1000 hours

🎮 Mechanics Needed

  • Consistent aerials
  • Faster gameplay
  • Some wall play

📈 To Rank Up → Champion

  1. Fix rotation mistakes
  2. Improve first touch
  3. Start reviewing replays

Note: Diamond is where players plateau because mistakes become positional, not mechanical

👑

Champion

1055–1294 MMR

⏱️ Estimated Hours

1000–2000 hours

🎮 Mechanics Needed

  • Strong aerial control
  • Basic air dribbles
  • Solid defense

📈 To Rank Up → Grand Champion

  1. Master game sense
  2. Master boost management
  3. Master consistency under pressure
  4. Reduce mistakes (this matters more than mechanics now)
⭐

Grand Champion

1295–1714 MMR

⏱️ Estimated Hours

2000–4000+ hours

Top ~1%+ of players

🎮 Mechanics Needed

  • Advanced mechanics (flip resets, fast aerials)
  • Elite positioning
  • High-speed decision making

📈 To Rank Up → Supersonic Legend

Near-perfect execution required:

  1. Rotation
  2. Speed
  3. Decision-making

Pro Tip: Play smarter, not flashier

🚀

Supersonic Legend

1715+ MMR

⏱️ Estimated Hours

4000–8000+ hours

Elite top ~0.05%

🎮 Mechanics Needed

  • Perfect consistency
  • Advanced mechanics under pressure
  • Pro-level game sense

📈 Progression

No next rank — leaderboard based
